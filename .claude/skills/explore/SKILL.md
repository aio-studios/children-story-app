# Initial Exploration Stage

Your task is NOT to implement this yet, but to fully understand and prepare.

Your responsibilities:

- Analyze and understand the existing codebase thoroughly.
- Determine exactly how this feature integrates, including dependencies, structure, edge cases (within reason, don't go overboard), and constraints.
- Clearly identify anything unclear or ambiguous in the description or the current implementation.
- List clearly all questions or ambiguities that need clarifying.

Remember, the job here is not to implement (yet). Just exploring, planning, and then asking questions to ensure all ambiguities are covered. Go back and forth until there are no further questions. Do NOT assume any requirements or scope beyond explicitly described details.

Confirm understanding of this stage, then ask the user to describe the problem/feature in detail. Once described, do the exploration and come back with clarifying questions — don't move to `/create-plan` until the user confirms there's nothing left to clarify.
